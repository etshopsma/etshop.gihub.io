⚡ Key Rules & Shortcuts for HSC

Master these patterns and you will solve 80% of preposition questions in HSC exams.

Rule 1 — Feelings use "OF"

Emotions and mental states: afraid of, fond of, proud of, ashamed of, jealous of, aware of

Rule 2 — Comparison uses "TO"

Relating or comparing: similar to, inferior to, superior to, equal to, prior to, next to

Rule 3 — Skill uses "AT" or "IN"

Abilities and expertise: good at, expert at, skilled in, experienced in, interested in

Rule 4 — Addiction/Attachment uses "TO"

Dependence or dedication: addicted to, devoted to, dedicated to, committed to, accustomed to

Rule 5 — Responsibility uses "FOR"

Accountability: responsible for, liable for, sorry for, grateful for, eligible for

Rule 6 — Surprise uses "AT"

Reaction words: surprised at, astonished at, amazed at, shocked at, angry at/with

Rule 7 — People use "WITH", things use "AT"

angry with (a person) vs angry at (a situation); pleased with a person, pleased at news

Rule 8 — "Consist of" vs "Consist in"

consist of = made up of (parts); consist in = lies in (an idea/quality). Very common HSC trap!

🎓 Exam Day Tips — Last Minute Revision

🔥 Most Repeated in HSC

fond of · addicted to · accustomed to · superior/inferior to · responsible for · familiar with · succeed in · consist of · differ from · suffer from · aware of · capable of

⚠️ Common Traps

die of (disease/hunger) vs die from (wound/injury) · agree with (person) vs agree to (proposal) · angry with (person) vs angry at (situation)

📌 "IN" Patterns

Believe, succeed, take part, engage, interest, involve, result, persist, specialise — all use in

📌 "ON" Patterns

Depend, rely, insist, concentrate, comment, decide, count, act, embark, lay emphasis — all use on

📌 "FROM" Patterns

Suffer, recover, benefit, prevent, abstain, refrain, escape, differ, emerge, resign — all use from

📌 "FOR" Patterns

Famous, responsible, eligible, sorry, grateful, notorious, fit, ready, suitable, known — all use for

Prepared for HSC Students of Bangladesh · Class 11 & 12 · All Education Boards

Focus on the HSC-marked examples first — these appear most frequently across Dhaka, Chittagong, Rajshahi, Sylhet, Comilla, Jessore, Dinajpur and Mymensingh boards.
